Design Your House Today

The entire Ibn-Abdillah interior design team was truly amazing to work with. They fully captured our vision and need for functionality and gave us our dream home! They also made the process fun and exciting along the way and we trusted them completely. They truly are the best interior designers in Miami and would recommend them 100000%!!

Ibn-Abdillah Interiors is one of South Florida’s top residential interior design firms. Based in Miami, FL, it serves a high-end clientele in Florida, throughout the United States, and internationally. Founded in 2004 by Ivonne Ronderos, its award-winning Miami interior designers have had two decades of experience creating exceptional residential interiors for clients around the world.

About Us

We rely on our years of knowledge and experience in interior design to guide our clients through the design process, whether it’s home decoration, complete remodel, or new construction. Our skills and expertise are delivered with exceptional customer service. Every Ibn-Abdillah team member plays an integral role in the collaboration process to create an authentic style and identity for each of our clients’ residential spaces. Our Miami interior designers work with passion, know-how, and integrity, offering human-centered interior design services.

Our professional and energetic team of talented Miami interior designers and experienced project managers flawlessly guides clients through all types of residential design projects, from initial concept to a timely final execution. Our Miami interior designers take pride in creating luxurious and innovative homes that are designed specifically for their owners, reflecting who they are and how they live.
